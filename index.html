Clubs & Extracurricular Activities

Student clubs are an important part of campus life at Amrita Amaravati. They provide opportunities to explore interests, develop leadership skills, build friendships, and gain practical experience beyond academics. Whether you are passionate about technology, music, dance, sports, literature, or community service, there is a club for everyone.

⚡ Chakravyuha Technical Club

Coding competitions, hackathons, workshops, technical projects, innovation challenges, and skill development opportunities.

🤖 Robotics Club

Design, build, and program robots while exploring automation, electronics, and engineering innovation.

🎵 Saptaswara

The music club for students passionate about singing, instruments, performances, and musical events.

💃 Nrityasparsh

The dance club that brings together students interested in classical, contemporary, and modern dance forms.

📝 Prachurya

A platform for literature, creative writing, public speaking, storytelling, and communication skills.

🎭 Drsya📸

The drama and theatre club that encourages acting, stage performances, script writing, and creativity.

🏆 Avisruta

The sports club of Amrita Amaravati that promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship through various indoor and outdoor games, tournaments, and athletic events. n.

🎯 Academic Scholarship Slabs

🥇 Slab 1

CGPA Above 8.0

🥈 Slab 2

CGPA Above 7.5

🥉 Slab 3

CGPA Above 7.0

💡 Maintaining a higher CGPA can help students remain in higher scholarship slabs. Consistent performance in CIA exams, assignments, labs, and End Semester Exams plays an important role in achieving this goal.

📚 Tips to Maintain Your Slab

  • Study daily and avoid last-minute preparation.
  • Revise topics after every class.
  • Maintain at least 75% attendance.
  • Complete assignments and lab work on time.
  • Practice previous-year question papers.
  • Seek help from faculty and seniors whenever needed.
